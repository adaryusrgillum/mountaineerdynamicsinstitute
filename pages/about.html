Our Journey

How We Got Here

2019

MDI founded in Morgantown, WV with a single instructor and a passion for teaching responsible gun ownership.

2020

Record enrollment as new gun owners seek quality training. Expanded team and course offerings.

2022

Launched Church Security Program, training over 50 congregations in active threat response.

2024

Surpassed 1,000 students trained with a perfect safety record. Opened second training location.
